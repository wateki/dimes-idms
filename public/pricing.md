# Pricing — DIMES IDMS

Structured pricing for MEAL and humanitarian program teams. All paid plans support annual billing at ~10% discount.

## Free

- Price: $0/month
- Users: up to 2
- Projects: 1
- Forms/reports: 5 forms and reports
- Submissions: 500 form submissions
- Includes: Dimes Collect mobile app, email support, basic features
- Credit card: not required

## Basic

- Price: $150/month (annual ~$1,620/year)
- Users: up to 7
- Projects: up to 4
- Submissions: 2,500/month
- Includes: unlimited forms/reports, Dimes Collect, priority support, advanced features

## Professional

- Price: $400/month (annual ~$4,320/year)
- Users: up to 20
- Projects: up to 10
- Submissions: 10,000/month
- Includes: dedicated server instance, advanced analytics, custom integrations, priority support

## Enterprise

- Price: $800/month (annual ~$8,640/year)
- Users: unlimited
- Projects: unlimited
- Submissions: unlimited
- Includes: multi-region dedicated servers, SLA, custom training, dedicated support
